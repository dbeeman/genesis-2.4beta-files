Routine Name:	printargs

Description:	Displays its arguments with argument numbers.

Usage:		printargs(argument)

Example:	genesis > printargs "a b c d"
		argc 1 : a,b,c,d
		genesis > printargs a b c d
		argc 1 : a
		argc 2 : b
		argc 3 : c
		argc 4 : d

Notes:

See also:	argc, argv, arglist, getarg
